Welcome

Since beginning my discipleship walk with the Lord, I had had a desire to share the love of Christ with others. The purpose of this blog is to bring people closer in their relationship with Jesus, as well as give a glimpse of the nature of God as it's been revealed in Scripture. It is a collection of my personal a-ha! moments, as well as prayers of worship.

What this blog is

A commentary of Scripture that seeks to accurately handle the word of God and remain faithful to the teaching of sound doctrine, acknowledging that we are saved by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone.

What this blog isn't

An attempt to validate Scripture with extra-biblical accounts, or some scientific belief, nor be some moral guidelines on how to live a "better", "healthier" lifestyle by putting your faith in Christ.
